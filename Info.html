POPULAR QUESTION

AI Doctor is your healthcare system. We harness the power of technology to bring together a wide range of healthcare services in a single integrated platform.

From diagnosis, consultation, claims management to appointment booking, AI Doctor is the ideal system for all your healthcare needs.

With our system, healthcare is now simpler and more convenient. We make it easier for you to access and manage your personal health to optimize health care in your busy life.

Currently, we help you quickly diagnose your health condition and quickly connect to a network of consultants, book an appointment for a medical examination at a convenient time for you. You can use AI Doctor anywhere, whenever you need.

Diagnostic and health consulting services are provided 24/7, depending on the needs of customers. This means you can call using AI Doctor at any time. However, if no doctor can see you after five minutes, don't worry we will contact you as soon as possible.

Anyone who cares about their health can use AI Doctor App.

For our diagnostic service, anyone with a non-emergency medical condition is eligible. However, those under the age of 16 must have a parent present during the online counseling session.

You must first visit the AI Doctor web link. Next, the website will display a login window and choose to log in through your Facebook account, Google account or fill in the details yourself. Creating an account is free - you'll only be charged when you book a hospital or clinic appointment.

To update your details, log in on the website and click the "Person" box at the top right of the screen and select the item you want to update. Once you've finished updating, click the "Save" button and your details will be updated.

Of course, we take security very seriously. Our platform uses end-to-end encryption to keep your information secure.

Our patient data and medical records are compliant with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). These records can only be accessed by healthcare providers directly involved in patient care and to support internal processes.
